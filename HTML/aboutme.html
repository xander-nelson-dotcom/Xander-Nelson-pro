About Me

Who I am, what drives me, and where I’m going.

Hello! I'm Xander — a driven, people-oriented leader with a passion for branding, marketing, and helping teams excel. I love building experiences, improving systems, and understanding what makes people move.

What I'm Passionate About

  • Creating meaningful experiences that leave people better than before.
  • Understanding people — what drives them, how they think, and how to truly connect.
  • Creativity and storytelling, especially in how brands shape culture and emotion.
  • Leadership that builds people up and helps them grow in real, lasting ways.
  • Becoming a better version of myself and pursuing growth in every part of life.

Where I'm Going

As I progress academically and professionally, I hope to build meaningful connections and leave a positive impacts wherever work. I have set my professional goals to be focused on careers that would seemlessly involve brand management and public facing corporate roles, likely leading to positions such as chief brand officer. Regardless of whatever company I'm apart of, I want to see people as people, not numbers, and how we can improve their in impactful ways.
